# What Matters

- Start with the problem, not the solution.
- Have a metric to evaluate success.
- Favor workflow over autonoy.
- work bottom up, not top down.
- Start simple, and then add them together.
- Start with large frontier models, then reduce.
- Think context rather than memory.
- Most problems are soloved with prompts.
- Look at the traces.
- Be a scientist, not shortcut to research and development.
